Natty Fitness is a user-friendly fitness website promoting health and wellness. It offers tips on workouts, diet plans, mental wellness, and more — all in one place.

🌟 Features
Video Banner with tagline: "Transform your sweat into strength"

Sections for:
Workout Tips
Diet Plans
Bulking & Cutting Tips
Calm Mind Practices
Team Section with certified fitness experts
Login and Feedback buttons for easy access
Fully responsive design for all devices

📁 Main Files
index.html: Homepage
workout.html: Workout guides
dietPlanForm.html: Diet plan form
bulkinTipsPage.html, cuttingtipsPage.html: Fitness tips
calmMind.html: Mental wellness
home.css, home.js: Styling and interactivity
assets/: Images & videos
